Who We Are

Rivers are not problems.
They are systems.

Pravāh was founded on a simple belief: that rivers don't just need cleaning — they need restoration at a systems level. Pollution, encroachment, reduced flow, broken ecology — these are engineering and governance problems that need engineering and governance solutions.

We combine technology and science to develop restoration techniques, while simultaneously building a community of people who care deeply about their rivers and act on that care.

Let's give our rivers another life.

— Pravāh Mission Statement

How Pravāh Operates

One mission.
Two wings.

We believe river restoration needs both rigorous science and people power. Pravāh is structured around two complementary wings that work together.

TECH
Wing 01 — Technology
Tech & Research Wing

Developing science-backed techniques, tools, and systems for river restoration. This wing treats rivers as engineering problems and builds solutions that can scale across India.

🔬
Field Research & Surveys
Systematic origin-to-confluence river mapping, water quality testing, flow measurement
📊
Data & Diagnostics
GIS mapping, hydrological modeling, open datasets for researchers and policymakers
⚙️
Restoration Techniques
Developing bio-remediation, natural flood management, and urban recharge solutions
🏛️
Policy & Advocacy
Translating research into actionable policy briefs and institutional partnerships

Social & Community Wing

📢
Awareness Campaigns

How We Work

Science meets
community action.

01 — RESEARCH
🔬
Field Documentation
Systematic tracking of rivers from origin — mapping course, measuring flow, documenting encroachment and ecological health with scientific rigor.
02 — ANALYSIS
📊
Data & Diagnostics
Water quality testing, hydrological modeling, GIS mapping. Building open datasets that researchers, government, and citizens can use freely.
03 — ENGINEERING
⚙️
Restoration Solutions
Developing bio-remediation, natural flood management, and urban recharge structures — tested, documented, and designed to scale.
04 — ACTION
🌿
Ground Cleanup Drives
Organised, data-tracked cleanup events as part of a sustained action plan — not isolated days, but a continuous restoration effort on the riverbanks.
05 — COMMUNITY
🌍
People Network
Building a worldwide network of volunteers, students, engineers, and river advocates — people who stay invested, not just show up once.
06 — SCALE
🗺️
Replicable Model
Everything we build for Saraswati and Kanh is a blueprint. One city, two rivers, one model — designed to be replicated across India.
