Privacy Policy

Simple privacy notice.

This is a starter privacy policy for the current site. It covers the volunteer form, general site use, and the basic data we may collect through direct contact.

What we collect

When you submit the volunteer form, we may collect your name, email address, city, LinkedIn or portfolio link, chosen wing, role, and any message you choose to share.

If you email us directly, we will also receive the details you send in that email.

How we use it

We use form submissions to respond to volunteer interest, coordinate field work, and keep in touch with people who want to support Pravāh.

We do not sell personal data. We also do not use it for unrelated marketing.

Payments and donations

Donation payments are not active yet. When Razorpay goes live, this page should be updated to explain what payment data is handled by Razorpay and what data Pravāh keeps for receipts and donation follow-up.

Contact

For questions about this policy, write to contact@pravah.online.
