Building Pravāh's ground network

A clear look at the people needed on the ground, from field researchers and engineers to outreach volunteers and policy support.

Who we need

Pravāh needs people who can handle field work, data, and coordination. That includes survey support, mapping, documentation, cleanup planning, and communication work.

We are also looking for people who understand civil engineering, environmental systems, and local policy work. The project is stronger when technical and social work move together.

How to join

The volunteer form on the home page is the main entry point. We keep the process simple so the right people can join without extra friction.
