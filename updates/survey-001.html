Field Report

Survey 001: Mapping the Saraswati and Kanh

The first systematic field survey of both rivers. We walked the full stretch, noted blockage points, and collected samples for a first unified map of the system.

What we saw

The survey covered the main visible stretches of the Saraswati and Kanh inside Indore. The goal was simple. See the rivers as one connected system, not as separate names on paper.

We documented access points, obvious obstruction zones, signs of flow change, and the places where the river edge needs the most attention.

What comes next

  • Build a cleaner map of the full route.
  • Compare sample results with location notes.
  • Use the survey data to plan the next field visit.
